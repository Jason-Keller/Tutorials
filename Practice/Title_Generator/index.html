Title Inflation - It Can Happen to Anyone

What is title inflation?

Title inflation is the not-so-subtle art of inflating titles without meaning -- and without a corresponding increase in pay. The Atlantic brings up an important point: the call for "extremists" in otherwise menial roles. You want a customer service "rockstar"? Pay your "rockstar" like a rockstar, and you'll get a rockstar. What many recruiters, hiring managers and "HR prophets" often forget is that the person on the other end of the interview is human; we're prone to nervous smiles, trembling hands, fumbling words, and, let's face it, a lack of life-alterting passion for some roles. So no, we're not "horny for sales." We're not "dying" to get to fix your codebase. We want to work -- ideally at a job we're actually passionate about, earning enough to live comfortably if we so desire, and still have some semblance of a life outside of our careers as... "Software Rockstars.""
